About Me

The Gist

Coding really began for me in college. I watched in amazement as my friends wrote programs to solve their everyday problems. After bombarding them with questions, they took it upon themselves to show me the basics, and the Internet became my playground.


Fast-forward some years. Now I develop professional websites for a living. At the moment I craft topnotch sites at Catnap with my partner Kyle. Music is also a really big part of my life. I will be graduating soon with a degree in Music and Computer Science.


So if you'd like to grab a beer sometime and talk about Life, the Internet, or a fresh idea be sure to hit me up at liam@liamscott.me
